**Problem Statement**

**Singapore HDB Resale Price Analysis (2017–2024)**

**Purpose**
This document outlines the key business and analytical questions addressed in this project.
All analysis, KPIs, and dashboards are designed to answer these questions in a clear and actionable way.

●	Analyze long-term price trends from 2017 to 2024

●	Identify periods of acceleration, slowdown, or volatility

**Metrics Used:**

●	Median resale price

●	Year-over-Year (YoY) growth %
