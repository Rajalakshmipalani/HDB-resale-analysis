**Analytical & KPI-Support Columns**

transaction_count ->	Used in aggregated views to represent volume

median_resale_price ->	Median resale price used for KPI calculations

yoy_growth_pct ->	Year-over-Year growth percentage based on median price

value_retention_index ->	Ratio of median price of older flats to newer flats

________________________________________________________________________________________________

**Considerations**

●	Median values are preferred over averages due to right-skewed price distributions.

●	Flat age is calculated approximately and does not account for lease top-ups.

●	Renovation quality and interior condition are not captured in the dataset.
